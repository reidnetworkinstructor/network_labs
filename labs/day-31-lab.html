Threat Actor Profile Report

Objective: Research and document a threat actor group to understand their motivations, tactics, and attack techniques.

Step 1: Pick a Threat Group

  • Select from: APT29, Lazarus Group, FIN7, LAPSUS$, Wizard Spider
  • Or choose any well-documented group

Step 2: Investigate and Document

  • Name & Origin: Where is the group believed to operate from?
  • Primary Targets: Industries or regions most affected
  • Motivations: Financial, political, espionage, etc.
  • Attack Methods: Phishing, ransomware, zero-days
  • MITRE ATT&CK Techniques: Example: T1059 - Command and Scripting Interpreter
  • Recent Campaign: Summarize one high-profile attack
  • Sources: List URLs or articles used

Step 3: Create a One-Page PDF

  • Use Google Docs, Canva, or any tool you like
  • Include visuals such as threat actor logos or MITRE matrices
  • Organize clearly and save as a PDF
