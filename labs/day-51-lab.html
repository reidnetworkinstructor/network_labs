Step 2: Your First Searches (Built-in Data)

Even with no external data, Splunk has internal logs you can explore.

  • Run: index=_internal
  • Skim the event list. Click a few events and observe default fields like _time, host, source, sourcetype.
  • Refine:
    • Only show warnings/errors:
      index=_internal (error OR warn)
    • Filter by sourcetype:
      index=_internal sourcetype=splunkd

Step 3: Explore Fields

  • Open the Fields sidebar (left). Note Interesting Fields and their top values.
  • Click a field (e.g., log_level) to filter results by a value.

Step 4: Summaries with SPL

Use transforming commands to turn raw events into insights.

  • Count by sourcetype:
    index=_internal | stats count by sourcetype
  • Top sources (top 5):
    index=_internal | top source limit=5
  • Events over time (hourly):
    index=_internal | timechart span=1h count
