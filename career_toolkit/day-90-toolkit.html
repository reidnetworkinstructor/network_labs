Day 90 Toolkit

Final Launch Day

Copy‑paste scripts, schedules, and templates to power your application sprint with speed and consistency.

Pair with: Day 90 Checklist

Use the Copy buttons to grab text quickly. Keep this page open while applying.

1) Launch Day Schedule (Blocks)

Block 1 (45m) — Skill searches & shortlisting
• Run 3–4 keyword searches (e.g., "Network+", "Security+", "AZ‑900", "Active Directory", "TCP/IP", "Splunk")
• Save roles posted in last 7 days that match ~70%+

Block 2 (60m) — Tailor & apply (5–7 roles)
• Paste ATS DOCX into portals; attach one‑page PDF if allowed
• Move matching keywords to top of Summary/Skills
• Link portfolio + top GitHub repo

Break (10m)

Block 3 (45m) — Outreach & referrals
• Send 5 short notes to recruiters/contacts
• Post concise LinkedIn launch update

Block 4 (20m) — Tracker & follow‑up tasks
• Log all applications
• Set follow‑up reminders for 7–10 days

2) LinkedIn Launch Post (Short)

Launch day: I’m applying to entry-level [Help Desk / SOC / Cloud Support] roles today. Portfolio + projects here: [link]. Certifications: [Network+ / Security+ / AZ‑900]. If your team supports [users / SOC ops / cloud reliability], I’d love to connect.

3) Recruiter DM (After Apply)

Hi [Name] — I just applied to [Job Title, Job ID] at [Company]. I’m [Network+ / AZ‑900] with labs in [skill]. One‑page resume + portfolio: [links]. Happy to share more context if helpful. Thanks for your time.
Hi [Name], I’m targeting entry‑level [role]. My background: [certs], projects in [skills]. If there are upcoming roles on your team, I’d appreciate a quick chat. Portfolio: [link].

4) Email Subject Lines

Subject: Entry‑level [Role] — [First Last], [Cert]
Subject: Application for [Job Title, Job ID] — [First Last]
Subject: Referral Request — Entry‑level [Role] (Portfolio inside)

5) Follow‑Up Note (7–10 Days)

Hi [Name] — checking in on my application for [Job Title, Job ID]. I can speak to [requirement] and have relevant projects here: [link]. Appreciate any next steps you’d recommend.

6) Skill‑Based Search Strings (Copy to job boards)

("CompTIA Network+" OR Network+) AND ("Active Directory" OR "ticketing system" OR troubleshooting)
("Security+" OR "CompTIA Security+") AND (SIEM OR Splunk OR "log analysis" OR "incident response")
("AZ-900" OR "Azure Fundamentals") AND ("Microsoft 365" OR "Azure AD" OR identity OR helpdesk)
("TCP/IP" OR DNS OR DHCP) AND ("help desk" OR "it support" OR "service desk")

7) One‑Page Resume Quick Builder (Copy Blocks)

Summary — Entry‑level [Role] with [Certs] and hands‑on labs in [skills]. Projects: [1–2]. Strengths: troubleshooting, documentation, clear communication.
Projects (1–2 lines each)
• Tiny Log Analyzer — speeds failed‑login triage from ~15m → <2m. Tools: Python, regex | GitHub: [link]
• Network Mapping — discovery + traceroute write‑up; improved lab documentation. Tools: Windows CLI | GitHub: [link]

8) Phone Screen Prep (2‑Minute Warm‑Up)

• 60s “Tell me about yourself” (role, certs, projects, target)
• 30s “Why this company/role?”
• 60s project highlight with result
• One technical headline (help desk / SOC / cloud)
• One question for them about success metrics

9) Voicemail Script (Recruiter/Manager)

Hi [Name], this is [First Last]. I applied to [Job Title] and would love to connect. I’m [certs] with projects in [skills]. My number is [phone]. I’ll follow up by email with my one‑page resume and portfolio link. Thank you.

10) Rejection Reframe (Reply)

Thanks for the update, [Name]. I appreciate your time and would value any feedback on strengthening my fit for future roles. If a junior [role] opens, please keep me in mind — I’m continuing to build projects in [skill].

12) Daily/Weekly Cadence (After Launch Day)

Daily (45–60m):
• Run 3 skill searches → shortlist 3 roles
• Tailor ATS resume and apply to 2–3
• Log apps, send 1–2 outreach notes

Weekly:
• Update resume keywords, add new projects
• Rotate search strings
• Follow up on pending apps (7–10 days)
• Book one peer mock or study block

Reminder: Apply when you match ~70% of the description, especially the top requirements. Titles vary; skills travel.
