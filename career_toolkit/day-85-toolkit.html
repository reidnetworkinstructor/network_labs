10) Testing Your Resume

1. Save as ATS_Master.docx (plain text formatting only).
2. Upload to a free ATS checker (Jobscan, Resumeworded).
3. Verify each section parses correctly.
4. Adjust keywords and section titles as needed.
5. Save a lightly formatted copy for human readers.
