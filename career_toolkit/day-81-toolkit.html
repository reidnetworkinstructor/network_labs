Day 81 Toolkit

Building Your Tech Brand

Use these templates, swipe files, and tools to implement your Day 81 checklist—fast.

Pair with: Day 81 Checklist

Tip: Use the Copy buttons to grab templates and paste them directly into LinkedIn, GitHub, or your portfolio.

1) Elevator Pitch Templates

Choose one, fill the brackets, and read it aloud until it feels natural.

I am a [role/credential] with [skills/experience], and I help [audience] achieve [result/outcome]. I’m targeting [specific role/team] where I can [impact you drive].
I specialize in [domain/stack]—recently [project/accomplishment]. I’m seeking a role in [company/industry] to improve [metric/problem] through [your approach/tools].

2) Headline Swipe File (LinkedIn / Portfolio)

Mix and match. Keep it concise and role-focused.

Network Technician | Security+ | Troubleshooting & Uptime
SOC Analyst (Security+) | Threat Monitoring & Incident Response
IT Support Specialist | AZ-900 | Microsoft 365 & Endpoint Management
Junior Cloud Support | AZ-900 | Cost, Reliability, Identity
Cybersecurity Analyst | Log Analysis | Splunk Core User
Help Desk | Ticket Triage | Customer-first Problem Solver

3) About/Summary Starters

Use these as a base for LinkedIn “About” or portfolio bios.

I’m an entry-level IT professional with hands-on labs in networking, cloud, and security. I’ve built projects that map networks, analyze logs, and harden configurations. I’m excited to support a team where documentation, reliability, and customer experience matter.
My focus is practical problem-solving: clear runbooks, fast triage, and measurable improvements. I’ve earned [certs], contributed to [projects], and I’m targeting roles in [help desk / SOC / cloud support] where I can reduce time-to-resolution and improve uptime.

4) GitHub README Template (Profile or Project)

# Project Title
Brief, results-focused description (what it does and why it matters).

## Highlights
- Problem: [what you solved]
- Approach: [tools/techniques]
- Result: [outcome/metrics if any]

## How to Run
1) Steps to install
2) Steps to execute
3) Expected output

## What I Learned
- [skill/insight]
- [skill/insight]

## Next Steps
- [clear, realistic improvement]

8) Before/After Transformation Script

Use this when you record a short profile update video or post a status update.

Before: My profiles were inconsistent and didn’t show my work.
After: Clear headline, pinned projects with READMEs, and a simple portfolio linking everything.
Result: More profile views, recruiter messages, and easier intros.
